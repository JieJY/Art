Meet the Founder

Artist: Alexander Zou

Born: 1951, Taiwan

Current Residence: Sydney, Australia

Education

Graduated from the Art Department, University of New South Wales (2013) and Visual Arts Department, TAFEN Nepean University, Sydney, Australia (2006–2010).

Professional Experience

Instructor at the Printmaking Workshop, Essmoi Fondazigne Modigliani, Rome, Italy. Painter at Penrith Regional Gallery & The Lewers Bequest, Sydney, Australia.

Collections

His works are collected in Nepean College of TAFE, Nepean Art Center (Sydney), and private collections in Taiwan and Italy.

Awards

Alexander has been continuously selected for the Sydney Blacktown Art Prize and Fisher’s Ghost Art Award between 2009–2016, gaining recognition as a "Master of Postmodern Spiritual Art."

Exhibitions

He has exhibited in numerous galleries around the world, including solo exhibitions in Rome and group exhibitions in Sydney, New Zealand, Vietnam, and Taiwan.

Artistic Philosophy

His art blends Buddhist and Daoist philosophies with Western postmodernism, creating a unique style that reflects both Eastern and Western thought.

World Traveler

A passionate solo traveler, Alexander enjoys exploring ancient civilizations. His journeys have inspired his art, deepening his cultural understanding.
