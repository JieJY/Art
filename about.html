About the Art Village

Founded by the visionary artist Alex Zou, our art village is a transformative project inspired by his recent work in Yunnan Province. After a distinguished career as a professional artist in Australia, Alex Zou began creating unique abstract sculptures in Yunnan, blending Western styles with Chinese philosophical influences. These sculptures have inspired the concept for our international art village, designed to foster collaboration among global artists and ignite creativity in new generations of Chinese artists and art students.

The art village will be situated in a key location within China, anchored by a grand iconic building and four smaller sculptural structures that form the village's core. Each of these structures will function as both a workspace and a striking visual element, reflecting the synergy of diverse artistic traditions. Resident artists from around the world will contribute their unique works, enhancing the village's postmodern environment.

Upon completion, the village will also include an art university, set to partner with both Chinese and international institutions. This university will attract art students from across China and beyond, offering them the opportunity to engage with and learn from esteemed foreign artists. The academic program will include workshops and educational exchanges, further enriching the cultural experience of the village.

We expect our art village to be a major arts and culture destination, renowned for its distinctive design, the diverse contributions of resident artists, and the unique chance for visitors to meet international art figures. With its innovative artistic approach, cultural exchange, and engaging activities, our art village is poised to make a significant impact both within China and globally, providing a life-changing experience for all involved.
