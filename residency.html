Residency Program

We are excited to announce our Art Residency Program, set to take place in Alex Zou's art village in China starting in 2025. This three-month residency offers artists from around the world the chance to engage deeply with their preferred artistic mediums, creating work that reflects their unique vision while utilizing the diverse materials provided by our art village.

Situated within the village’s iconic sculptural structures, which double as functional studio and gallery spaces, each resident artist will have the freedom to explore and develop their craft in a setting that merges art and architecture seamlessly. This residency also presents a unique platform for artists to interact with Chinese art students and local artists through potential workshops and collaborative events, fostering a dynamic exchange of techniques and perspectives. As part of China’s initiative to enrich its cultural landscape and facilitate cross-cultural learning, this program aligns perfectly with the country’s ambition to expand its cultural industry and deepen its engagement with global artistic practices.

Artists will not only experience the vibrant tapestry of Chinese culture but will also benefit from a supportive and accommodating environment tailored to ensure a smooth adaptation to their new surroundings. The art village team is dedicated to providing a comfortable and enriching residency experience, making this an unparalleled opportunity for personal and professional growth within a global artistic community.
